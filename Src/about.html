About Me

Timothy Moss was born in Toowoomba, QLD in the year 2000. He lived in Toowoomba up until he was 3 years old, where he was then diagnosed with Juvenille Type 1 Diabetes.

He then moved to the Sunshine Coast with his family and continued to live there for most of his childhood and teenage life. He went to primary school at Caloundra State Primary. After grduating from Primary School, he then went on to complete his secondary education at Caloundra State High.
